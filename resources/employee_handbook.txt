1. LEAVE POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Annual Leave: All permanent employees are entitled to 21 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. 
Casual Leave: 8 days per year. Cannot be carried forward to the next year.
Sick Leave: 12 days per year with medical certificate required for more than 2 consecutive days.
Maternity Leave: 26 weeks paid leave as per the Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act 2017.
Paternity Leave: 5 days paid leave to be taken within 30 days of child's birth.
Public Holidays: 12 national/festival holidays as declared annually by the company.

Leave Application: All leave must be applied through the HR portal at least 48 hours in advance (except emergencies).
Leave Balance: Can be checked on the employee self-service portal.

2. WORK FROM HOME POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

WFH Eligibility: Employees with > 6 months tenure in non-client-facing roles.
WFH Days: Maximum 2 days per week. Must be pre-approved by reporting manager.
Core Hours: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM IST must be available for collaboration regardless of work location.
Equipment: Laptop, VPN access, and internet allowance of ₹1,000/month provided.

3. CODE OF CONDUCT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

All employees must maintain professional behavior, respect colleagues, and uphold company values.
Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or bullying.
Conflict of interest must be disclosed to HR within 7 days.
Confidential company information must not be shared externally.

4. BENEFITS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Health Insurance: Mediclaim cover of ₹5 Lakhs per employee + 2 dependents.
Group Term Life Insurance: 3x Annual CTC.
Provident Fund: 12% employer contribution.
Gratuity: As per Payment of Gratuity Act.
Learning & Development: ₹25,000/year for courses, certifications, or conferences.
Meal Allowance: ₹3,000/month (non-taxable).
Internet Reimbursement: ₹1,000/month for WFH-eligible employees.

5. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Reviews are conducted biannually: April and October.
OKRs (Objectives & Key Results) are set at the start of each quarter.
Promotions are considered annually in March based on performance scores.
Rating Scale: 1 (Below Expectations) to 5 (Exceptional).

1. RECRUITMENT PROCESS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Stage 1 — Job Requisition:
  - Department head raises a job requisition via HR portal
  - HR validates headcount budget with Finance
  - JD (Job Description) drafted within 3 business days

Stage 2 — Sourcing:
  - Primary: LinkedIn Recruiter, Naukri, company careers page
  - Secondary: Employee referrals (₹25,000 referral bonus for successful hire)
  - Target TAT (Time-to-Hire): 30 days for junior, 45 days for senior roles

Stage 3 — Interview Process:
  Round 1: HR screening (30 min)
  Round 2: Technical assessment or case study
  Round 3: Panel interview with team lead
  Round 4 (senior roles): Leadership interview with VP/CXO

Stage 4 — Offer & Onboarding:
  - Offer letter issued within 2 business days of final approval
  - Background verification via AuthBridge (7–10 days)
  - Onboarding kit dispatched before Day 1

5. DISCIPLINARY PROCEDURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step 1: Verbal Warning (documented in HR system)
Step 2: Written Warning (formal letter issued)
Step 3: Performance Improvement Plan (30–60 days)
Step 4: Termination (subject to Legal & HR review)

All disciplinary actions require HR Business Partner sign-off.
Appeals can be filed within 7 days of the action to the CHRO.
